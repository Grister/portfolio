Air Alert Analysis
The Air Alert Analysis project is a data processing and visualization tool that analyzes air alert notifications across different regions and timeframes. The data is stored in JSON format and is visualized through interactive bar charts. The data is taken from kaggle.com. The project highlights the frequency of alerts by region and by month.
